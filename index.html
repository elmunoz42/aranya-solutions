Nonprofit Mission: Aranya Solutions' purpose is to enrich the quality of life within targeted economically challenged and developing areas such as Nepal. Aranya Solutions develops systematic changes with a focus on localization serving as a bridge to resources and providing necessary infrastructure. Our method is to assist communities to implement environmentally friendly building and agricultural methods, life skills workshops, local business development, leadership training, education initiatives, and capacity building.

Empowering Villagers to Preserve Their Community and Culture

Village Community Support

We work closely with the villagers to identify specific village needs, and strive to source or offer the needed resources and support. Examples us such support is providing training to diversify the agriculture, extending growing season, improving livestock raising techniques, education initiatives, and creating value added products.

  • Identify existing resources and needs
  • Fill in the gaps
  • Work with the community
  • Create lasting social and economic impact

Leadership Training

Our focus is to empower leaders in the village, especially women to come forward with renewed confidence in their capacity to make a positive change in their community.


  • Emotional intelligence training
  • Governance and communication skills
  • Literacy and general education when appropriate

Small Enterprises and Cooperative:

Initiatives are created with assistance from other successful programs and suggestions for income generation using local resources, such as honey production.

The education and economic empowerment of women has been established as the cause for later marriages and pregnancies, better economic outcomes and the strengthening of the social fabric.


  • Creation of value added products
  • Strengthening the cohesion of the community
  • Sense of pride and respect at home
  • Developing relationships with local markets
